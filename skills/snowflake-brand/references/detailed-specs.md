## Logo Specifications

### Variants
- **Snowflake Blue logo**: For white and dotted/patterned light backgrounds
- **White logo**: For Snowflake Blue backgrounds, color-overlay images, and non-white specialty items

### Clear Space
Minimum clear space on all sides equals the x-height of the Snowflake logotype

### Registration Mark
- The Snowflake bug (icon) must always include the (R) registration mark
- When enlarged: registration mark is one-third the size of an arrow leg
- Placement: at the center point of the 2 and 3 o'clock arrows' intersection

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not distort, stretch, or skew
- Do not rotate
- Do not recolor or apply color effects
- Do not add shadows, glows, or outlines
- Do not place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- Do not use outdated logo versions

## Typography Details

### TEXTA (Headline & Display)
- Primary heading font
- Use Heavy weight for buttons and CTAs
- Use for navigation labels and display text
- Licensed font — may not be available in all environments

### Lato (Body & Subhead)
- Available via Google Fonts (free)
- Weights available: Light (300), Regular (400), Bold (700), Black (900)
- Google Fonts URL: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Lato
- CSS import: `@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Lato:wght@300;400;700;900&display=swap');`
